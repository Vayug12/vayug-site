Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last Updated: February 25, 2026

1. Cancellation Policy

Advertisers can cancel scheduled ad campaigns through the Ad Management dashboard. If cancelled before the campaign starts, the remaining budget will be retained as a non-withdrawable advertising credit balance or refunded to the original payment method upon explicit request.

2. Refund Eligibility

Refunds are processed under the following circumstances:

  • An advertisement was rejected by our moderation team.
  • Technical failure on our platform prevented the ad from being served.
  • Double payment due to technical issues on the payment gateway.

3. Non-Refundable Items

Any budget already consumed for impressions, clicks, or video views is strictly non-refundable. Service fees, once consumed, are also non-refundable.

4. Refund Timeline & Process

To request a refund, email support@snehayog.site with your Transaction ID. Once approved, refunds are initiated within 5–7 working days and credited back to the original payment method.

5. Duplicate Payments

In case of duplicate payments, the extra amount will be automatically refunded within 72 hours without requiring a manual request.

6. Delivery Policy (Digital Services)

Vayug provides digital advertising services. There are no physical goods involved. Ad credits or campaign activations are delivered instantly upon successful payment. Users receive an automated confirmation email and see updated credits in their dashboard immediately.
