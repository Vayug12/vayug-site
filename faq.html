Frequently Asked Questions

Answers about Vayug's video experience, creator publishing, advertising, monetization, AI features, and privacy.

Vayug is a creator-first video platform for discovering, watching, publishing, and monetizing short-form and long-form video.
Eligible creators earn from advertising associated with eligible content. The configured revenue split is 80% to the creator and 20% to the platform, subject to eligibility, ad availability, policy, and payout requirements.

No. Unlike some platforms, Vayug does not require a minimum number of subscribers or watch hours before creators can start earning from eligible content.
