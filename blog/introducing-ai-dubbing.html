Introducing AI Dubbing in Hindi and English

Product Update July 15, 2026 Vayug Team

We're excited to announce AI-powered dubbing on Vayug, available now in Hindi and English. This feature helps creators reach wider audiences by transcribing, translating, and synthesizing speech for their videos.

How AI Dubbing Works

The dubbing pipeline on Vayug includes:

  • Transcription: Automatic speech-to-text generation from your video's audio track.
  • Translation: AI-powered translation between Hindi and English.
  • Speech Synthesis: Natural-sounding voice generation in the target language.

Who Benefits

AI dubbing is especially useful for:

  • Creators targeting audiences in different language regions
  • Educational content that needs to reach Hindi and English speakers
  • Entertainers looking to expand their viewer base

Availability

AI dubbing is available when the relevant AI engine and feature flags are enabled. Language support, voice quality, quotas, and processing time depend on the active AI provider and deployment configuration.

Get Started

Open the Vayug app, upload a video, and look for the dubbing option in the upload settings. Select your target language and let the AI handle the rest.
