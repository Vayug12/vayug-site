How Creator Monetization Works on Vayug

Monetization May 10, 2026 Vayug Team

One of the most common questions we get from new creators is: "How do I earn money on Vayug?" Here's a clear breakdown of how creator monetization works on our platform.

The 80/20 Revenue Share

Vayug's configured rule is an 80% creator / 20% platform split of eligible advertising revenue. This means that when ads are served on your eligible content, 80% of the revenue from those ads goes to you.

Important: This is a revenue-share percentage, not a guaranteed income, CPM, RPM, or promise that every video will receive ads.

What Affects Your Earnings

  • Content eligibility: Your content must meet platform policies and ad-friendliness guidelines.
  • Ad inventory and targeting: Ad availability depends on campaign targeting and user context.
  • Views and engagement: More views and engagement generally lead to more ad impressions.
  • Region: Ad rates vary by geographic location.
  • Policy compliance: Content that violates policies may not be eligible for monetization.

Setting Up Payouts

To receive payouts, you'll need to complete KYC verification and set up your payment information in the Creator Dashboard. Payouts have a minimum threshold and a clearing period before they become eligible for withdrawal.

Tracking Your Revenue

The Creator Dashboard provides detailed analytics including ad breakdowns, impressions, per-video performance, and lifetime creator rewards. Use these insights to optimize your content strategy.
