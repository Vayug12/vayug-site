Export Subscriber Emails with CSV Download

Creator Tools March 15, 2026 Vayug Team

Building a direct relationship with your audience is essential for long-term creator success. That's why Vayug now lets you export subscriber contact information as a CSV file from your Creator Dashboard.

What's Included in the Export

The CSV export includes the following subscriber information:

  • Subscriber name
  • Email address
  • Profile information
  • Subscription date

How to Export

  1. Open the Vayug app and navigate to your Creator Dashboard.
  2. Find the subscriber export section.
  3. Tap the export button to download your CSV file.
  4. Use the downloaded file with your email, newsletter, or CRM tools.

Privacy & Consent Requirements

When using exported subscriber data, you agree to:

  • Use subscriber emails only for legitimate communication related to your content.
  • Not sell, share, or transfer subscriber lists to third parties.
  • Provide an unsubscribe mechanism in external communications.
  • Comply with applicable data protection and anti-spam laws.

Why This Matters

Platform algorithms can change, but a direct email list is yours. Export your subscriber contacts to maintain relationships outside the Vayug platform, share updates, and build a sustainable creator business.
