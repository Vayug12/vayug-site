E2EE Subscriber-Only Content is Now Live

Feature Launch June 20, 2026 Vayug Team

We're thrilled to announce that end-to-end encrypted (E2EE) subscriber-only content is now live on Vayug. Creators can now publish exclusive videos that only their authorized subscribers can decrypt and watch.

Why E2EE Matters

Privacy and exclusivity are important for creators who want to offer premium content to their most dedicated subscribers. With E2EE, the video content is encrypted on the creator's device before upload, ensuring that only authorized subscribers can access it.

How It Works

  • The creator encrypts the video and its decryption key on their device.
  • The creator app generates encrypted key material for each authorized subscriber.
  • The backend stores encrypted per-subscriber keys (never the raw decryption key).
  • The subscriber's app decrypts the video locally using their authorized key.

Getting Started

To use E2EE, mark your video as subscriber-only during the upload process. Select which subscribers should have access, and the system will handle the encryption and key distribution automatically.

Security Notes

E2EE is designed for private or exclusive creator content. Effective security also depends on device security, key handling, and authentication. Always follow best practices for protecting your devices and accounts.
