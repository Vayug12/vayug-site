No Mid-Video Interruptions: Our Viewing Experience

Platform April 5, 2026 Vayug Team

When we designed Vayug, one of our core decisions was how to handle advertising. We chose to avoid mid-video interruptions entirely, and here's why that matters for both creators and viewers.

The Problem with Mid-Video Ads

Mid-video ad interruptions break the viewing flow. They disrupt emotional engagement, cause viewers to abandon videos, and can negatively impact creator retention metrics. We wanted to build a platform where content comes first.

Our Approach: Banner & Carousel Ads

Vayug uses banner and carousel ad placements instead of interrupting video playback. This means:

  • Viewers watch content without interruptions
  • Creators' videos maintain their intended pacing and impact
  • Advertisers still get visibility through non-intrusive placements
  • The overall experience feels more focused and enjoyable

What This Means for Creators

When your content qualifies for monetization, ads are displayed in non-disruptive formats. Your storytelling, education, or entertainment isn't interrupted — the viewer stays engaged with your content.

What This Means for Viewers

You can enjoy short-form discovery in Yug and long-form content in Vayu without the frustration of mid-video ad breaks. The focus stays on the content you chose to watch.
