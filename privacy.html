Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 11, 2026

Vayug is a digital content hosting and advertising platform that allows users to upload and share user-generated content (UGC). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information in compliance with global privacy standards and Google Play policies.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Information: Name, email address, profile information, and device identifiers.
  • User-Generated Content (UGC): Videos, images, captions, and related metadata uploaded to the platform.
  • Location Data: With your explicit permission, we collect your device's precise location to provide localized content, advertising, and community features. You can enable or disable location services at any time.
  • Advertising Data: Information related to advertising and campaign activity.
  • Transaction Information: Payment confirmations and billing records processed through authorized third-party payment gateways. We do not store card or banking details on our servers.
  • Reward Program Information: Additional verification details from eligible participants in the Creator Incentive Program to prevent fraud and abuse.
  • Moderation & Safety Data: Reports, flags, and related information submitted to maintain community safety.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To operate, maintain, and improve the Vayug platform.
  • To host, distribute, and moderate user-generated content.
  • To provide localized content and advertising.
  • To process advertising and calculate performance metrics.
  • To calculate platform-based engagement rewards under the Creator Incentive Program.
  • To detect fraud, abuse, or policy violations.

3. Creator Incentive Program

Vayug operates a discretionary Creator Incentive Program. Incentives are calculated based on platform performance metrics such as views and engagement levels. Incentives are platform-managed benefits and are not guaranteed earnings, salaries, employment compensation, or financial investment returns.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with:

  • Authorized payment gateways for transaction processing.
  • Cloud hosting providers for secure data storage.
  • Regulatory authorities when legally required.
  • Subscriber Information Sharing: When you subscribe to a creator, your name and email address will be shared with that creator for legitimate communication. You may unsubscribe at any time.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard security measures including encryption and access control to protect user data. Sensitive financial information is handled only by certified third-party payment processors.

6. Cookies & Tracking Technologies

We use cookies, secure tokens, and local device storage to maintain user login sessions, ensure platform security, analyze app performance, and deliver relevant advertisements.

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA)

Vayug is not intended for use by children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone under 13. If we discover a user under 13 has provided personal data, we will delete it and terminate the account.

8. User Rights & Data Deletion

Users may request access, correction, or deletion of their personal information. Account Deletion: You can delete your account within the Vayug app via Settings, or by contacting support@snehayog.site. We will purge your data within 30 days.
