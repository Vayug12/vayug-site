Get the App

Focused Viewing Without Disruptive Interruptions

Vayug is designed for a calm video experience across short-form discovery and long-form viewing, with a focus on avoiding disruptive mid-video interruptions.

▶️

Watch in Your Flow

Discover short videos in Yug or spend time with long-form content in Vayu, with playback built around the content.

🤝

Clear Creator Support

Vayug supports creators through advertising without making a promise that every viewer or video will have identical ad treatment.

⚡

Fast Video Delivery

Video processing and adaptive HLS playback support reliable playback across changing network conditions.

🔄

Keep Watching

Watch history and playback-position recovery help viewers return to content without losing their place.

Advertising Availability Varies

"Ad-free" and "no-interruption" describe the intended focused viewing experience and the avoidance of disruptive mid-video interruptions. Banner, carousel, or other ad placements may appear in some surfaces based on inventory, campaign targeting, content eligibility, user context, and deployment configuration. Check the current app experience and terms for the latest behavior.
