About Vayug

Vayug is a next-generation digital content hosting platform designed to empower creators across the globe. Our mission is to provide a high-performance environment where creators can share their creativity, engage with audiences, and viewers enjoy content without disruptive mid-video advertisements — all in one seamless experience.

Our Vision

We believe that content creators deserve a platform that prioritizes their growth and engagement. Vayug leverages cutting-edge technology to ensure fast video distribution and an interactive user experience.

What We Do

  • Content Hosting: Reliable hosting for short-form and long-form video content.
  • Creator Rewards: An engagement-based reward system to incentivize high-quality content production.
  • Digital Advertising: A robust advertising portal for businesses to reach targeted audiences.
  • Innovation: Constantly evolving with features like AI dubbing, E2EE content protection, and advanced analytics.

Open Source

The Vayug repository is MIT-licensed. Production deployments, user data, private credentials, media, and third-party provider accounts are separate from the open-source codebase.
