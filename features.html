Everything Creators Need to Grow

Vayug combines video publishing, audience growth, monetization, AI tools, and privacy features in one platform.

💰

Creator Monetization

Earn up to 80% of eligible ad revenue. No subscriber or watch-time thresholds required to start earning.
🎬

Creator Tools

Upload short and long-form videos, organize series, manage your profile, and access analytics.
🔐

E2EE Subscriber Content

Protect exclusive videos with end-to-end encryption. Only authorized subscribers can watch.
🎯

No Mid-Video Interruptions

Focus on your content. Vayug uses banner and carousel ads instead of interrupting playback.

Platform Features

Built for creators, viewers, and advertisers.

🌐

AI Dubbing

Transcribe, translate, and dub your videos in Hindi and English using AI-powered speech synthesis.

📧

Subscriber Email Export

Export subscriber names, emails, and profiles as CSV for your own newsletters and communication.

🔔

Direct Subscriber Alerts

Send push notifications to all or selected subscribers with optional links and performance tracking.

Creator Analytics

AI Agent Uploads

Embeddable Player

Yug — Short Videos

Vayu — Long Videos
