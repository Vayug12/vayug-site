Creator Monetization

Vayug connects creator publishing, audience growth, advertising, analytics, and payouts in one creator-first platform.

Configured Creator Share

80% of eligible advertising revenue goes to eligible creators. 20% is allocated to the platform.

This percentage is a revenue-share rule — not a guaranteed income, CPM, RPM, or promise that every video will receive ads.

💰

Ad-Supported Earnings

Eligible ad impressions and views associated with eligible content contribute to creator revenue accounting.

🎁

Lifetime Creator Reward

Creator Dashboard shows total lifetime creator rewards, current-period performance, and redeemable amounts.

📊

Revenue Analytics

Review ad breakdowns, impressions, per-video performance, views, shares, and audience insights.

🏦

Payout Workflow

Complete payment setup and satisfy account, content, regional, policy, and minimum-payout requirements.

📈

Audience Growth

Build a profile, publish content, grow subscribers, and use direct notifications to bring audiences back.

📢

Advertiser Ecosystem

Advertisers create campaigns, use targeting, and measure impressions and clicks on the platform.

What Affects Earnings?

  • Content and account eligibility
  • Ad inventory, campaign targeting, and user context
  • Views, impressions, engagement, and policy compliance
  • Region, payment setup, payout status, and applicable terms
